BNCC

BNCC stands for Bangladesh National Cadet Corps, a tri-service volunteer reserve defense force for students in schools, colleges, and universities. The BNCC is made up of the Bangladesh Army, Navy, and Air Force. The BNCC's mission is to train students to become good citizens who can serve the country as volunteers during peace and war. The BNCC's motto is "Knowledge & Discipline" and its spirit is "The Volunteers".
